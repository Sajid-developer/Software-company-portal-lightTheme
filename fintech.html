M-Cash operates similarly to a traditional banking system but leverages the flexibility and immediacy of mobile technology. M-Cash is a cutting-edge mobile commerce platform designed to seamlessly integrate with users' mobile handsets, offering a wide range of financial services with unparalleled convenience and security. At the heart of M-Cash is the virtual mWallet, an innovative digital account created for each registered user. This mWallet allows customers to manage their finances effortlessly—whether depositing funds, transferring money to other users, recharging mobile accounts, or checking their balance.

This means that users can conduct financial transactions swiftly and securely, from the palm of their hand. The system facilitates an array of functionality including cash-in, cash-out, peer- to-peer transfers, merchant payments, and bill payments.

How Fintech Service Works?

The M-Cash system's functional architecture is structured around several key elements:

  • System Actors: This includes all entities interacting with M-Cash, from individual users like subscribers and agents to third-party applications such as the Amal Express remittance platform.
  • Access Channels: Detailed descriptions of how users connect to the system via USSD, SIM App, and Web interfaces.
  • Wallets and Accounts: Information on the types of virtual wallets and accounts available within the system.
  • Registration Process: Steps required for users to set up their mWallet and access services.
